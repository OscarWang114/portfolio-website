LISA: Levels Integrated Secretarial Agent

LISA is a desktop managerial application created for life insurance agents to document the complex relationship between clients and contractors.

Source Code

This is a five-person team project completed in a software engineering course. My main contributions include:
1. Created the life insurance component.
2. Created functions for adding, storing, and reading life insurances.
3. Designed an original model for expressing one-to-many relationships between a person and life insurances using JavaFX data structures.
4. Created tests and documentation for all the enhancements I made.

About

Introduction

Hello! I'm Oscar Wang. I'm an undergraduate student at Waseda University, majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. I've also participated in a one-year exchange program at the National University of Singapore.

I love to build things that fulfill the needs of life, and I'm very excited about how human-centered design and intelligent machines can make a positive impact on our society. In addition to achieving excellent academic records at Waseda University, I seized precious research opportunities, undertook multiple internships at tech companies, and self-initiated a project where I lead a team to develop a user-friendly web application with over 4,000 returning users and 300K page views. All these endeavors cultivated my enthusiasm for creating human-centered intelligent systems, and I aspire to lead teams of passionate members to build innovative products in the future.
